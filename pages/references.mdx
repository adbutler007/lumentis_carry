# References

<Callout type="info">
  This section provides a list of works cited in the whitepaper, allowing readers to explore the research and ideas that inform the analysis in greater depth.
</Callout>

The following references are cited in the whitepaper:

1. Asness, Clifford S, Tobias J Moskowitz, and Lasse Heje Pedersen. 2013. "Value and Momentum Everywhere." *The Journal of Finance* 68 (3): 929–85.

2. Bakshi, Gurdip, Xiaohui Gao, and Alberto G Rossi. 2017. "Commodity Futures Prices: More Evidence on Forecast Power, Risk Premia and the Theory of Storage." *The Review of Financial Studies* 30 (10): 3337–82.

3. Baltas, Nick. 2017. "Optimising Cross-Asset Carry." *Available at SSRN 2968677*.

4. Baz, Jamil, Nicolas Granger, Campbell R Harvey, Nicolas Le Roux, and Sandy Rattray. 2015. "Dissecting Investment Strategies in the Cross Section and Time Series." *Available at SSRN 2695101*.

5. Boons, Martijn, Fernando M Duarte, Frans de Roon, and Marta Szymanowska. 2020. "Time-Varying Inflation Risk and Stock Returns." *Journal of Financial Economics* 136 (2): 444–70.

6. Frazzini, Andrea, and Lasse Heje Pedersen. 2014. "Betting Against Beta." *Journal of Financial Economics* 111 (1): 1–25.

7. Fuertes, Ana-Maria, Jo"elle Miffre, and Georgios Rallis. 2010. "Tactical Allocation in Commodity Futures Markets: Combining Momentum and Term Structure Signals." *Journal of Banking & Finance* 34 (10): 2530–48.

8. Gorton, Gary B, Fumio Hayashi, and K Geert Rouwenhorst. 2013. "The Fundamentals of Commodity Futures Returns." *Review of Finance* 17 (1): 35–105.

9. Ilmanen, Antti. 2011. "Expected Returns: An Investor's Guide to Harvesting Market Rewards." 

10. Koijen, Ralph SJ, Tobias J Moskowitz, Lasse Heje Pedersen, and Evert B Vrugt. 2018. "Carry." *Journal of Financial Economics* 127 (2): 197–225.

11. Moskowitz, Tobias J, Yao Hua Ooi, and Lasse Heje Pedersen. 2012. "Time Series Momentum." *Journal of Financial Economics* 104 (2): 228–50.

12. Szymanowska, Marta, Frans De Roon, Theo Nijman, and Rob Van Den Goorbergh. 2014. "An Anatomy of Commodity Futures Risk Premia." *The Journal of Finance* 69 (1): 453–82.

These references span seminal papers and recent research on topics such as the economic drivers of carry premia, the optimization of carry strategies, and the interaction of carry with other risk premia like value and momentum. Readers interested in a deeper understanding of the concepts and evidence behind the carry strategies discussed in this whitepaper are encouraged to explore these resources.
